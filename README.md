# Executive Summary

Manufacturing companies generate enormous amounts of production data every day. Unfortunately, raw data alone does not improve productivity. The real value comes from transforming machine events, downtime logs, maintenance records, and production metrics into meaningful business intelligence.

This portfolio demonstrates how manufacturing data can be converted into interactive dashboards, analytical reports, and decision-support tools using modern analytics technologies including Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Rather than simply displaying charts, the projects focus on solving real manufacturing problems such as:

- Equipment downtime
- Production losses
- OEE improvement
- Maintenance optimization
- Production reporting
- Root cause analysis
- Reliability engineering
- Executive KPI reporting

---

# Business Problem

Manufacturing organizations lose millions each year due to:

- Unplanned downtime
- Poor equipment reliability
- Inefficient maintenance scheduling
- Lack of KPI visibility
- Production bottlenecks
- Quality losses

Without proper analytics, managers often react to problems after they occur instead of preventing them.

This portfolio demonstrates how manufacturing analytics helps organizations make faster and better business decisions.

# Manufacturing KPIs

## Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)

Measures total manufacturing efficiency.

OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality

---

## Availability

Measures equipment uptime.

---

## Performance

Measures production speed versus ideal speed.

---

## Quality

Measures good parts versus total production.

---

## MTTR

Mean Time To Repair

Average repair duration after failures.

---

## MTBF

Mean Time Between Failures

Measures equipment reliability.

---

## Downtime

Tracks production interruptions.
